> [Blr Birders](https://blrbirders.com/) (earlier BNG Birds) is the
  Birdwatchers' Field Club of Bangalore. It is a forum for the exchange of
  information including notices, etc. pertaining to the birds and other
  wildlife of the city of Bangalore in India, and its environs. They organize
  bird walks across Bangalore.
